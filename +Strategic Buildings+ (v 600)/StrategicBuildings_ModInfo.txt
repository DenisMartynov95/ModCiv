[h1]Adds 3 Strategic Buildings and a Strategic Wonder. [b]Standalone![/b][/h1]
[h1]All of these features are an integral part of [url=steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2280231911]Core Mod[/url].[/h1]
[quote]
[h2][b]Refined Coal Facility[/b][/h2]
Provides [b]1 Coal[/b], as well as [b]+1 Production[/b] and [b]+1 Gold[/b] from worked Coal resources.
Limited to [b]3 Cities per empire[/b]. [b]Cannot be captured[/b].

[h2][b]Oil Refinery[/b][/h2]
Provides [b]1 Oil[/b] and [b]+2 Gold[/b] from worked Oil resources.
[b]Limited to 4 Cities per empire[/b]. [b]Cannot be captured[/b].

[h2][b]Nuclear Reprocessing Plant[/b][/h2]
Provides 5 [b]Uranium[/b] and [b]+3 Production[/b] from worked Uranium resources.
Requires a [b]Nuclear Plant[/b] in the city and is limited to [b]2 Buildings per empire[/b]. [b]Cannot be captured[/b].

[h2][b]Shandan Horse Farm - World Wonder[/b][/h2]
Provides [b]2 Horses[/b] and [b]+1 Production[/b] from worked Horse resources.
Requires the [b]Stables[/b] building. [b]Cannot be captured[/b].

[h2][b]Ironworks[/b]*[/h2]
Now also provides [b]3 Iron[/b].

[h2][b]Recycling Center[/b]*[/h2]
Now also grants [b]+2 Production[/b] in the City and [b]+2 Science[/b] from worked Aluminum resources.
[b]Cannot be captured.[/b]

[hr][/hr]*[i]Changes to existing Buildings can be disabled by deleting the [b]StrategicBldgs_Update.sql[/b] file.[/i]
[/quote]
